The importance of challenging yourself

There is often a lot of chatter about getting out of one's comfort zone and, more often than not, it is seen as a buzz phrase. It has become clich√©.

There are some things where it may not appear cool (because no one sees you) to others but it may be important to challenge yourself.

  • Cleaning up your messy apartment: No one will know unless you invite them in.
  • Your flexibility: No one will know unless you are in a yoga class
  • Appreciation of alone-time: No one will know that you enjoy spending time on your own. Others won't be partaking in your fun.

Here's a list of things I did on my own in chronological order the past year:

  1. Learn to ice skate outdoors
  2. Try to dance tango

The things I did were by no means easy but the important thing to note is to go out there and explore.
